Hi, I'm Kerem Kayabay

I am a researcher and developer interested in data science and information systems. My active research is about finding ways to help transform data-disadvantaged (or underachiever) organizations to use data more effectively. Whenever I find the time, I also develop open-source projects that address some of my problems: I want more control over the data and functionality. I also want to become more conscious of how I manage my attention while using any tool.

Hi, I'm Kerem Kayabay

I am a researcher and developer interested in information systems and data science. My current research is about finding ways to help transform data-disadvantaged (or underachiever) organizations to use data more effectively. I also use and develop open-source projects to solve some of my problems.

In this blog, you will find my latest findings. Sometimes I write here just to clarify my thoughts.

You can check out my research at Google Scholar and my projects at GitHub.

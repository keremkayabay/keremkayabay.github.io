Hi, I'm Kerem Kayabay

I am a researcher and developer. My goal is to empower people and communities.

My academic interest is mainly about data-driven organizations. I research how businesses create and use data products. I am also interested in how they manage their technology stack to support data science projects.

Whenever I find the time, I build open-source projects. These projects address the problems I have in the first place: I want to own and control my data. I also want to become more conscious of how I manage my attention while using any tool.

Here, I share my latest findings. You can check out my research at Google Scholar and my projects at GitHub.

Hi, I'm Kerem Kayabay

I am a researcher and developer. Through my work, I wish to empower people and communities.

Academically, I am interested in data-driven organizations. I research to answer questions like:

  • How businesses use data products: insights, recommendations, or visualizations?
  • How do they manage their technology stack for data science projects?
  • What can help transform traditional organizations to use data more effectively?

Whenever I find the time, I develop open-source projects. These projects address the problems I have in the first place: I want to own and control my data. I also want to become more conscious of how I manage my attention while using any tool.

Here, I share my latest findings. You can check out my research at Google Scholar and my projects at GitHub.

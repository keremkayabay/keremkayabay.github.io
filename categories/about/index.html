Hi, I'm Kerem Kayabay

I am a researcher and developer interested in data science and information systems. My active research is about finding ways to help transform data-disadvantaged (or underachiever) organizations to use data more effectively. Whenever I find the time, I also develop open-source projects that address some of my problems. Here, I share my latest findings. You can check out my research at Google Scholar and my projects at GitHub.

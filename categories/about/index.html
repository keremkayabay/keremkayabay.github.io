Hi, I'm Kerem Kayabay

I am a researcher and developer. Through my work, I wish to empower people and communities. Academically, I am interested in data-driven organizations. Here are some of the questions I try to answer: How businesses create and use data products (insights, visualizations, recommendations)? How do they manage their technology stack to support data science projects? Can a roadmapping technique help transform traditional organizations to use data more effectively? Whenever I find the time, I build open-source projects.

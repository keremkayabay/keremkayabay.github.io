Planning vs Preparation

You do planning to design the set of actions to achieve a goal. You do preparation to get ready for some future goal. Both are important. But the famous quote goes: “Everybody has a plan until they get punched in the mouth.” If your plans need to change during execution, the number of required modifications, and how well you implement those modifications depend on your preparation.
